Yar

Yarmouk University was established in 1976. It is a public university known for its strong programs in humanities and sciences. It includes colleges such as Arts, Science, Education, Economics, and IT

JUST

Jordan University of Science and Technology was established in 1986. It is a public university that focuses on scientific and medical fields. It includes around 11 colleges, such as Engineering, Medicine, Science, and IT.

JO

The University of Jordan was established in 1962. It is a public university and the first university in Jordan. It includes many colleges such as Engineering, Medicine, Science, Arts, Law, and Business.
